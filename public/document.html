Quick Start
What is notiFLY ?
A push notification management system for internal use by the marketing department of a company.
What are Member, Segment, and Campaign ?
  • Member: Refers to the members of your company. They are people whom you can reach via your collected member database.
  • Segment: Is the target audience of campaigns. Targeted communication based on member portfolio achieves better results and member experience. You can create your own Segment based on different campaign purposes.
  • Campaign: The notification campaign you want to send to your members. When creating a notification campaign, you must select a segment, which refers to the groups of members you set up in the Create Segment section beforehand. This enables you to send targeted notifications to specific groups, improving the precision of your marketing efforts.
What is System User ?
System User : People who have access to notiFLY. Note that only admin role could create new user account. For any needs, contact admin in your company.
How could Team collaborate better with notiFLY ?
Based on our experience, here is some tips we recommend.
  • Naming : when naming Segment or Campaign , the best practice is : “campaignName_createdate_channel_authorName”. For example: ChristmasLimitedOffer_20230412_webpush_Alison. Clear naming can help you quickly retrieve and analyze performance results in the future

    Why & How to import member ?
    Once your company decides to use the notiFLY system, you first need to synchronize your company's member data with notiFLY.
    There are two methods for updating the data. You can either upload a CSV file to insert all member data ( We do provide an "example CSV" for you to download) Alternatively, IT team could utilize an NPM package and API key to programmatically update the data to the notiFLY database.

    What is Delivery Type ?
    • One-time Delivery: You send this campaign only once, and it will end after that.
    • Periodic Delivery: You will send the same message in a regular basis.
    Remember, when you choose periodic delivery, interval and end_time are required. Interval means the message will be sent out every interval days. If interval is set to one, it means the message will be sent at the same time every day until the end_time is reached.
